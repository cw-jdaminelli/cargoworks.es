The Problem

Urban logistics is inefficient

Cities like Barcelona are dense, busy, and increasingly difficult for delivery vehicles to navigate. Traffic congestion, limited parking, restricted access zones, and short delivery windows make traditional courier services inefficient for short-distance urban deliveries.

Vans often spend more time searching for parking or navigating traffic than actually completing the delivery.

Traffic congestion slows everything down
Limited parking in city center
Restricted access zones
Short delivery windows missed
The Solution

Built for city deliveries

Cargo bikes are designed specifically for dense urban environments. They can move quickly through city streets, access areas where vans struggle, and complete deliveries without delays caused by traffic or parking restrictions.

For many deliveries within Barcelona, cargo bikes are simply the most efficient way to move goods across the city.
