We provide fast, reliable urban deliveries for businesses across Barcelona. Same-day jobs when something is urgent, and recurring routes when delivery is part of your daily operation.

CTAs
Get a quote / Book a delivery

---

What we do

Same-day deliveries  
When something needs to move today. Documents, parcels, food, flowers, spare parts, or last‑minute orders.

Recurring routes  
Daily or weekly routes, multi-stop runs, and regular city logistics designed to be predictable and easy to manage.

Van → cargo bike transition  
If you’re using vans inside the city, we help you move the right part of your operation to cargo bikes and make deliveries smoother over time.

---

Why cargo bikes

Barcelona is dense and busy. Vans get stuck. Cargo bikes keep moving.  
For many city routes, bikes are faster, easier to park, and more reliable — especially when there are multiple stops.

---

How it works

1. You send us the details (addresses, time window, load)
2. We confirm availability and handle the rest
3. You get clear communication and updates
4. The delivery is completed properly, with proof if needed

---

Responsibility

We take responsibility seriously:
- People: professional riders and realistic workloads
- City: fewer unnecessary vans on the street
- Environment: lower emissions as a result of good logistics

---

Talk to us.

ABOUT

Cargoworks is a Barcelona-based cargo bike courier service. We move things across the city every day, all year, in real conditions.

At our core, we are couriers. What supports that work is solid experience in cargo bike logistics: routes, timing, loads, pricing, operations, and people. We don’t just ride bikes — we understand how urban delivery works and take responsibility for doing it properly.

Cargo bike logistics isn’t a trend for us. It’s a professional tool.

We work with businesses that deliver regularly, as well as clients who need occasional same-day jobs. Some are moving away from vans, others start directly with bikes. In both cases, our role is to make deliveries simple, reliable, and realistic.

Every job is planned with care. Sometimes that means redesigning routes. Sometimes adjusting expectations. Often, it just means simplifying things.

We take responsibility towards our clients, our riders, the city, and the environment. Sustainability isn’t a slogan — it’s what happens when logistics are done well.

FAQ

Do you cover all of Barcelona?  
Yes. Coverage depends on the job, so send us the postcodes and we’ll confirm.

Do you handle multi-stop deliveries?  
Yes. Multi-stop routes are a big part of our work.

What can you carry?  
A wide range of city-friendly loads. Share approximate size and weight.

Do you offer same-day service?  
Yes, subject to availability and timing.

Can you replace vans completely?  
Sometimes. Often it’s a mix. We help find what makes sense.

How does pricing work?  
Pricing is clear and consistent, based on the delivery details.
